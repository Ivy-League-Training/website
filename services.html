MEWP / Aerial Lift Training

Mobile Elevating Work Platforms (MEWPs) are among the most common sources of workplace fatalities. Proper training is not just an OSHA requirement — it's a matter of life and death.

Our MEWP training meets ANSI A92.22 (Safe Use) and A92.24 (Training) standards, ensuring your operators understand both the machine and the hazards of working at height.

The Online Problem

Why Online Training Isn't Enough

OSHA's standard is clear: forklift training must include formal instruction, practical training, and a performance evaluation. A click-through course doesn't cover all three.

Formal Instruction

Online courses check this box — but often poorly, with generic content not specific to your equipment or environment.

Practical Training

Online training cannot provide hands-on operation. This is where most companies fall short — and where OSHA inspectors look first.

Performance Evaluation

Each operator must be individually evaluated on the actual type of truck in the actual workplace. No website can do this for you.
