Ivy League Training and Sales LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit getivyleague.com or contact us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it to us — primarily through our contact and quote request form. This may include:

  • Your name and the name of your company or organization

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through our contact form is used solely to:

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. How Your Form Submissions Are Processed

Our contact form uses Formspree (formspree.io) to securely transmit your submission to us via email. Formspree processes your data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We do not store form submission data on our website or on Cloudflare's servers.

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may log standard server access data (such as IP addresses and page requests) as part of their infrastructure operations. This data is handled in accordance with Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy. We do not have access to or control over Cloudflare's server-level logs.

5. Phone Numbers and Contact Information

Our business phone number is displayed on this website for the purpose of allowing you to contact us directly. We take reasonable steps to protect our contact information from automated harvesting by encoding it in a way that is readable by humans but not easily parsed by bots.

When you call us, we may retain your phone number and notes from the conversation in order to follow up on your inquiry. This information is not shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed to businesses and working professionals. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Retention

We retain contact and inquiry information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — typically the duration of a business relationship or inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly.
