Our Commitment

Seat Belts Save Lives — Especially on a Forklift

Forklifts tip over. It's not a question of if — it's when, and whether the operator survives it. A seat belt is the single most critical safety device on a forklift, and operator instinct to bail out is almost always fatal.

We emphasize this in every course we teach, because it saves lives. Our operators don't just know the rule — they understand why.

OSHA Forklift Facts

  • ~85 workers killed in forklift accidents annually in the US
  • 34,900 serious injuries per year involving forklifts
  • Overturns account for ~25% of forklift fatalities
  • Untrained operators are 3× more likely to be involved in an incident
  • OSHA requires training before first operation
  • Recertification required every 3 years minimum
